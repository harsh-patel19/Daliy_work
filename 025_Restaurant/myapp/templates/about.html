Who We Are

A kitchen built on tradition

Saffron & Stone started as a small family kitchen in Adajan, Surat. What began as cooking for neighbours grew into a full restaurant, driven by one simple belief — that food cooked with patience and the finest ingredients speaks for itself.

We work with local farmers, seasonal produce, and recipes passed down through generations. Every dish tells a story. Every plate carries the warmth of the people who made it.

Fresh & Local
